Coffee Shop Sales Analysis Dashboard - Excel

As a data analyst working at Maven Roasters, a coffee shop chain with three locations in New York City, I have been delegated by the franchise owner to understand customer purchasing behavior and streamline operations using transactional data from January to June 2023.
